Tutorial

Sometimes a third party will enter combat alongside Aubrey. In this situation, an asterisk (*) denotes which participants Aubrey can influence.

More powerful beings' stats start hidden from extra-dimensional scrutiny. Creatures brought into this world via Council resources will have fully readable stats, move sets, etc., by default.

Combat Menu: Ada

ATTACK
An inelegant flying tackle.

GUARD
Defends for one turn, reducing incoming damage by a minimum of 25%.

SPECIAL
Access Ada's elemental magics. SP Cost: 1 per element.

AUTO
Leave Ada to her own devices. Combat will resolve on its own with an unpredictable outcome.

Combat Menu: Green Book

ATTACK
Pathetic.

GUARD
Hopeless.

SPECIAL - THE DEX
Look up something, or someone's, stats. SP Cost: 1.

SPECIAL - INVIGORATE
Restores a random amount of SP to one target. SP Cost: 2.

SPECIAL - CONVERT
Turns any specified unit of SP into HP, and vice-versa.

SPECIAL - HEAL
Restores 5 HP to any target. SP Cost: 2.
